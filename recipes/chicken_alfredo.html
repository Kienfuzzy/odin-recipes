Chicken Alfredo

Description

Chicken Alfredo is a creamy pasta dish featuring tender pieces of chicken and fettuccine noodles coated in a rich Alfredo sauce made from butter, cream, and Parmesan cheese. It's a comforting meal that's quick and easy to prepare, making it ideal for busy weeknights.

This recipe can be customized with vegetables or other proteins to suit your preferences.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz fettuccine
  • 1 pound chicken breast, cooked and sliced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Parsley, for garnish

Steps

  1. Cook the fettuccine according to package instructions; drain and set aside.
  2. In a skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the garlic and saut√© for about 1 minute.
  3. Stir in the heavy cream and simmer for 3-4 minutes until slightly thickened.
  4. Add the Parmesan cheese, stirring until melted and smooth. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Toss the cooked fettuccine with the sauce and top with sliced chicken.
  6. Garnish with parsley and additional Parmesan cheese, if desired.
