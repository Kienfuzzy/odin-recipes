Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

Spaghetti Bolognese is a hearty Italian pasta dish made with ground meat, tomatoes, and a variety of seasonings. It's known for its rich flavor and satisfying texture, making it a family favorite. Serve it topped with freshly grated Parmesan cheese for an extra touch of flavor.

This dish is perfect for weeknight dinners or special occasions and can easily be doubled to feed a crowd.

Ingredients

  • 12 oz spaghetti
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, grated
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Parmesan cheese, for serving

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions; drain and set aside.
  2. In a skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  3. Add the onion, garlic, and carrot; saut√© until the onion is translucent.
  4. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. Toss the cooked spaghetti with the sauce and serve with grated Parmesan cheese.
